Who I Am

I’m a Robotics Engineer passionate about designing autonomous systems that bridge the gap between digital intelligence and the physical world. I build systems that don’t just think , they move, adapt, and solve real-world problems.

Currently, I’m diving deeper into the AI space, exploring Generative AI, Large Language Models (LLMs), and intelligent systems that are reshaping how humans interact with technology.

I’m a continuous learner powered by curiosity (and a slightly unreasonable amount of coffee ☕). If there’s something new to explore, build, or break-then-rebuild-better, I’m in! I’m hands-on by nature , a mix of kinesthetic and visual learning.

When I'm not building robots, you'll probably find me

  • Enjoying stand-up comedy (because debugging is easier when you can laugh about it 😄)
  • Traveling - exploring new places, food, cultures, and ideas ✈️

Currently based in France 🇫🇷 , building systems, ideas, and the occasional over-engineered weekend project.
